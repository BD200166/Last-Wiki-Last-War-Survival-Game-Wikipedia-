Heroes

You can improve your own heroes under the “Heroes” tab.
The level of the heroes can be improved with "Hero-EXP" and the military
Rank (hero-stars) can be improved with the help of so-called “fragments”.
These fragments are available as "SR fragments" (blue), "SSR fragments" (purple) and
"UR fragments" (orange) for the respective classes you need the respective fragments.

SR-Heroes (Blue)

Represents the lowest and weakest hero rarity, useful for beginners.

SSR-Heroes (Purple)

Represents medium and moderate hero rarity, useful for more advanced
players and sometimes also for very advanced players.

UR-Heroes (Orange)

Represents the highest and rarest hero rarity, useful for all kind of players,
the strongest heroes belong to this class.

Hero types

There are 3 different hero types => Tank | Aircraft | Rocket truck
and 3 different subclasses => Damage dealer | Damage absorber | Damage supporter

Tank | Damage dealer
Tank | Damage absorber
Tank | Damage supporter
Tank | recieves 20% less damage from Rocket truck

Aircraft | Damage dealer
Aircraft | Damage absorber
Aircraft | Damage supporter
Aircraft | recieves 20% less damage from Tank

Rocket truck | Damage dealer
Rocket truck | Damage absorber
Rocket truck | Damage supporter
Rocket truck | recieves 20% less damage from Aircraft


Heroes of the same type gain extra buffs:

3 heroes of same type => +5% HP, +5% Attack, +5% Defense

3 heroes of same type & 2 heroes of same type => +10% HP, +10% Attack, +10% Defense

4 heroes of same type => +15% HP, +15% Attack, +15% Defense

5 heroes of same type => +20% HP, +20% Attack, +20% Defense

Hero armor

There are 4 different types of hero armor => R | SR | SSR | UR
Max level of all components is level 40 (except UR) if your equipment factory has reached
level 20 you are able to upgrade UR armor beyond level 40.

R => most common rarity, weakest components, useful for beginners

SR => common rarity, weak components, useful for beginners

SSR => rare rarity, moderate/good components, useful for beginners and advanced player

UR => rarest rarity, best components, useful for every kind of player


World

You can access the world map under the “World” tab. You gain access to many different
functions via the world map such as locating or attacking zombies of any kind. You can also
find any type of players in order to spy and/or attack them and on top you can also find resource deposits.

Alliance

Under the “Alliance” tab you can find all functions related to the alliance to which you belong.
Here you will find, for example, functions such as picking up alliance gifts, contributing to alliance research,
the member list and so on.

Mail

Under the “Mail” tab you have access to all types of messages you can receive. alliance news,
private messages, congress messages etc. can all be found here. Also, many rewards are distributed through the
Mail system, so you should check here regularly.

Inventory

Under the "Inventory" tab you have access to all kinds of resources/treasures/supply boxes.
Every usable item is stored here and you can use them whenever and however you want. Protective shields are
also provided over the inventory menu.


Shop

Under the “Shop” tab you have access to various offers. Here you can find all kinds of commercially available game
content (can only be purchased with real money) and also daily free gifts, so it's worth checking in here every day.

Hot deals

You can find time-limited events under the “Hot deals” tab. These limited time events include
daily missions that you can complete to progress in the "Event Pass" and receive rewards. The
Event Pass is available in a free version and a paid version.

Events

Under the “Events” tab you will find a number of different events, some changing daily, others weekly.
The main events take place in this tab, including both individual events and alliance events.

VS/Alliance duel

Under the tab “VS/Alliance Duel” you can find all information regarding the respective alliance duel. Every week
a new alliance is matched with your alliance that duels with your own alliance over 6 days (Mon-Sat) and every day
has a different combat theme.


Energy

Under the "Energy" tab (the lightning bolt symbol at the top left of the screen) you will find your energy bar. The energy bar
shows you how much energy is still available (energy is used to carry out attacks of all kinds).

VIP

Under the "VIP" tab (top left of the screen under the lightning bolt symbol) you can find everything about VIP status.
VIP status grants you passive buffs of all kinds and daily free gifts. You can get VIP status for 30 days by purchasing it
for 10.000 diamonds (the best and most cost-effective offer).

Building

Under the "Building" tab (the hammer symbol at the top left of the screen) you can view the queue of buildings
that are currently under construction or being upgraded.

Research

Under the "Research" tab (the test tube symbol at the top left of the screen) you can view the queue of
research projects that are currently being researched.


Radar missions

Under the "Radar Missions" tab (the antenna/radar symbol at the bottom left of the world map) you can access your
radar missions. These radar missions are always refilled at a certain time interval and depending on the radar level
you can store a different amount of missions. Radar missions include, for example, helping alliance members find resources
collect resources for yourself or dig up treasures.

Chat

Under the "Chat" tab (the transparent field at the bottom middle between the "Heroes" and "World" tabs) you have
access to the game's various communication channels. From private chats to world chats you can find all communication
channels here.


Event Desertstorm

The "Desertstorm" event is a duel between two alliances on a separate map. The aim of this event is to attack and capture
various buildings and collect more points than the opposing alliance in a given time (approx. 40 - 45 minutes). In order
to take part in this event you need to send a battle request which you can find in the "Desertstorm" event tab. Good coordination
and arriving on time are the key to victory in this event.

Event Pioneer Training

The "Pioneer Training" event is about defeating pioneers. You can find them on the world map. You choose the level of
your pioneer training beforehand and then defeat pioneers from level 1-30. Teamwork is the key here, as the pioneers
gain a lot of combat power with each level, so it would be recommended that the alliance members help each other out.

Event Marshall Guard

The "Marshall Guard" event deals with deafeating the different levels of the "Marshall Guards". Togethers as an alliance
you attack these "Marshall Guards" over a period of time (approx. 30 minutes) to deal as much damage as possible. You need
to reach a certain damage number (based on the Marshall Guards level) in order to successfully finish this event.

Event Hawk Chopper

The "Hawk Chopper" event (accessible through the orange chopper in your base) has 2 different types of quests the "honorable quest"
and the "special operations". Both of these quests are solo quests (PVE) where you have to climb a level letter in order to recieve
different rewards. It is recommended to check in regularly.


Drone

The "Tactical Drone" is part of your hero squad an assists them in fights. It is basically an extra hero-unit of your squad with its own
abilitys and special attacks. You can upgrade your drone within the following tabs:

Attributes => Use Battle data and Drone parts to level up your drone.

Component => Upgrade your components to increase the drone attributes.

Skill Chip => Use and upgrade drone skills to further increase the drone attributes.

Tips & Tricks

You can find and collect daily free rewards in the VIP tab, Shop tab and you can collect 90 free diamonds by giving away 3 likes in the Arena tab.

Use your second troop in the "Marshall Guard" event to open up a rally, this way you can attack more times
with your first troop and deal more damage.

You can farm free troops and level for your heroes under the Hawk Chopper tab (the orange chopper in your base).
Under "honorable quest" you can farm level and rewards for your heroes and under "special operations" you can farm
troops and Hero-EXP.

Save up different tasks (such as radar missions, building upgrades, tech research etc.) to collect them on
particular days for the alliance duell in order to gain more points. (For instance save up your radar missions
over sunday so you can collect them on monday, monday is always day 1 of the alliance duell and the daily theme
for day 1 is radar special training). You can find the daily theme for each day under the "VS/Alliance Duel" tab.

How to alliance duel

Monday (Day 1)
[Radar-Training]

- Use stamina
- Complete radar tasks
- Use hero exp points
- Use drone combat points
- Use drone parts
- Gather food
- Gather iron
- Gather coins
- Collect data chip points


Tuesday (Day 2)
[Base Expansion]

- Use construction speedups
- Increase building power
- Dispatch legendary trade trucks
- Perform legendary secret tasks


Wednesday (Day 3)
[Age of Science]

- Use research speedups
- Increase tech power
- Use valor badges
- Complete radar tasks
- Upgrade drone components


Thursday (Day 4)
[Train Heroes]

- Use elite recruition
- Use hero exp points
- Use hero shards of any kind
- Use skill medals


Friday (Day 5)
[Total Mobilization]

- Complete radar tasks
- Use construction speedups
- Use research speedups
- Use training speedups
- Increase building power
- Increase tech power
- Train units of any kind


Saturday (Day 6)
[Enemy Buster]

- Dispatch legendary trade trucks
- Perform legendary secret tasks
- Use any kind of speedups
- Kill as many enemy units as possible


Sunday (Day 0)
[Rest day]

- Recovering from the alliance duel
- Prepare for the next alliance duel
